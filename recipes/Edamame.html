Edamame Recipe

Description

With this recipe you will be cooking some delicious edamame, a classic food from some asian countries.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of edamame
  • 1 pinch of fine salt
  • 1 liter of water
  • 1 pinch of salt flakes
  • 1 pinch of sweet paprika

Steps

  1. Boil some water and add a pinch of fine salt on it.
  2. Add the edemame bag in the boiling water. Let it boil for 5 minutes.
  3. Remove the edemame from the boiled water, let them cool.
  4. Add the edemame in a plate, add salt flates and sweet paprika.
